Game

Hangman

In this game, an everyday phrase e.g "bring home the beacon" will be represented by a row of dashes. Your task is to guess the phrase. If your guess is part of the phrase, the ' _ ' will be replaced. You will be assigned 6 chances to guess correctly - otherwise the game will be over.
To start: enter your nickname in the field below and save.


RULES
  1. Do not REFRESH or click the BACK button of your browser while playing the game.
  2. Only ALPHABETIC CHARACTERS are allowed
  3. 6 game lives may be too short for you. Will a hint help?
