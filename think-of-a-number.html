Game

Think of a Number

In this game, the computer will randomly generate a number between 1 and 100, and the number will be hidden from you. Your task is to guess and insert the guessed number in the input box.
When you click the 'Guess' button, a message indicating whether your guess is too high, too low or correct will be shown in a coloured box.


RULES
  1. Do not REFRESH your browser or Click the RESTART Button while playing.
  2. To start a new game, click the RESTART button.
